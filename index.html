IDENTITY

Samarra & Co. is a digital agency and art collective based in New York City, Paris, and Jeddah.

We work with startups, corporations, artists, fashion brands, hospitality groups, and other agencies in order to bring their ideas to life.

Perfectionism and creativity drive everything we do, from the music videos that we direct, to the applications that we create, and the advertising campaigns that we lead.
